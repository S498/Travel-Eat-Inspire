This is how It Goes at Travel || Eat || Inspire

1

A virtuous traveller, goes around a place and visits all the best places of the Location.

2

He eats hunts for all traditional eats at that Destination.

3

He contacts the Writers at Travel || Eat || Inspire, and tells about his experience at that particular destination.

4

The Writers writes a story and tallies it with the traveller if he felt the same way, when the traveller accept it the Writer publishes it.

5

The writers, work on the foods the travellers likes in that particular destination, they find out the traditional recipe and Post it in the Page.

6

The writers, ask the travellers why they want other travellers should visit that destination and what made them go to that place, and write an inspiring story about that place.
